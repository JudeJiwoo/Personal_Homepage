About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in Artificial Intelligence at Sogang University, Seoul, working under the supervision of Prof. Du-Seong Chang. My research focuses on Multimodal Large Language Models, Generative AI, and Music/Audio Information Retrieval.

Currently, I am leading a government-funded research project ($4.7M) on Video-to-Audio Generation as part of the IITP Professional AI Talent Development Program for Multimodal AI Agents. My recent work includes developing query-robust multimodal retrieval models and hallucination detection systems for Multimodal LLMs.

Beyond research, I have extensive experience in ML infrastructure management, having administered distributed GPU server clusters and containerized environments for our lab during my master's studies. I also contributed to national AI data construction projects with NIA.
